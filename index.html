Recipe

Chocolate Cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!"


Difficulty Preparation Baking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

 Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs and flour

3

 Mix everything to get a smooth mix

4

 Bake in the oven
